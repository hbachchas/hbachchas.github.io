Hi, Welcome!
I am a #computer-science #ai enthusiast. My main research interest is in applications of AI in healthcare and biomedicine. Currently, I am working as a senior lecturer at the University of Applied Sciences Krems in Austria. Before that, I was a postdoc at the University of Tromsø in Norway, where I worked with Prof. Dilip Prasad on an ERC project. Before joining UiT, I completed my PhD at IIT Roorkee in India, under the guidance of Prof. Balasubramanian Raman. During my PhD, I studied the recognition and generation problems in human motion. I completed my Master's under the direction of Prof. Gyanendra Verma at NIT Kurukshetra. Before that, I completed my Bachelor's in Computer Science from HBTI Kanpur.

Research Interests

My present research revolves around nanoscopy and self-supervised learning. I am interested in applications of machine learning, especially in the healthcare sector. My broad areas of interest are:

  • Healthcare Biomedical Applications
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Cognitive Architectures
